**About the Guest:**

[Marco](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marco-franzreb-578546176/) is a software engineer at NVIDIA, where he works on improving the [nvCOMP](https://developer.nvidia.com/nvcomp) library, which offers fast GPU implementations of multiple data compression formats. For the past couple of months he has been working on a GPU implementation of the rotate algorithm.
