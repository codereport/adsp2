**About the Guests:**

[Vittorio](https://romeo.training/) is a passionate C++ expert with over a decade of professional and personal experience. His expertise covers library development, high-performance financial backends, game development, open-source contributions, and active participation in ISO C++ standardization. He is the coauthor of "Embracing Modern C++ Safely" and is a speaker at over 25 international conferences.

[JF Bastien](https://jfbastien.com/) has worked on hardware, compilers, security, performance, web browsers, and airplanes. As chair of the C++ language evolution working group and co-designer of WebAssembly, his contributions have helped shape modern software development.
